The Best Movies According to Gaurav

My Top 4 favorite movies of all-time

Good Will Hunting

This is a really good movie of Robin Williams,he is one of my fav. actors.

Dead Poets Society

Another Robin Williams movie which takes your mind into places you have never been,truly mind broadening.

Into the wild

A movie based on travel but more on philosophical journey of a man who breaks boundaries fearlessly to explore the alaskan part of the US.

Tamasha

A very touching hindi bollywood movie showcasing the struggle of indian middle class mentality of living life.
